Study program Bachelor of ICT

Introduction

Western society is based on well functioning but rapidly changing technological applications. However, mere specialisation is no longer enough modern technologists need to be capable of forming a global overview of the developments in their branch. Accordingly, graduates from the Faculty of Technology of University Drenthe are broadly educated technologists with an eye for innovation, management and social circumstances. The Faculty of Technology offers a four-year study program Bachelor of ICT.

Course characters

Work is done on a problem-oriented, project basis, often involving external research, and the practical training periods are completed at interesting positions in the relevant industries.

Internship

In the third and fourth year students have the opportunity to put the theory into practice. A company based traineeship of five months in the third year is part of the program and in the fourth year students work on a final graduation project for one semester. Both periods can be spent in the Netherlands or abroad.
